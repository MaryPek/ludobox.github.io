Ludobox

Print, Craft, Play

Ludobox is an electronic device to distribute games from the public domain into the public space. It contains dozens of blueprints, documents and instructions to make your own games and turn any surroundings into a playful environment.
Print'n play are games that you can fabricate by yourself.
Paper, card decks, scissor, dices, 3D printer, laser cutter, etc.

Make your own Box

You can build your own box from scratch
or directly download games from our online collection.
